# Practice Brief

Day la mot repo gia lap theo kieu bai hackathon.

## Nhiem vu 1

Doc codebase va viet mo ta ngan cho cac cau hoi sau:

- He thong nay nhan du lieu dau vao gi?
- Luong match lenh chay qua nhung module nao?
- Cac quy tac uu tien gia va thoi gian dang duoc xu ly the nao?

## Nhiem vu 2

Tim va fix cac bug co chu dich duoi day:

1. Lenh market co hanh vi khong dung trong mot so tinh huong.
2. Gia giao dich dang khong duoc tinh theo quy tac mong doi cua san.
3. Snapshot order book sau khi match van con du lieu sai.

Goi y: khong can tin mo ta nay hoan toan day du. Nen doc test, chay CLI, va tu tao them case.

## Nhiem vu 3

Them 1 trong cac feature sau:

1. Ho tro cancel order theo `order_id`.
2. Ho tro IOC order.
3. Them endpoint/service de tra ve trade history.

## Nhiem vu 4

Sau khi sua xong, viet summary ngan:

- Nguyen nhan goc
- Cach fix
- Cach verify
- Trade-off neu co
